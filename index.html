About Us

BoatHead is a Web3 app with inbuilt Game-Fi elements rewarding users for their boat time as well as incentivising them to enjoy experience the Miami yachting lifestyle.

BoatHead NFT holders can level up their Boat Head NFTs and also receive Daily Tasks to earn additional rewards and tokens such as:

  • Have a 30 min Jetsky session.
  • Play a betting Battleship game on the water;
  • Have a 4 Hour Boat Party.
  • Do check in at sponsored marketplace;.

Received token rewards can be swapped to stable coin as well as provide I.R.L utility and access to special events, hard-to-reach places, and discounts on Boating-Related products and services promo deals from our partners such as gym memberships, wearables and much more.
