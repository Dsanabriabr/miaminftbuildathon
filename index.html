THE SPECS

Our dynamic NFTs (dNFTs) are more than just membership tokens. In addition to each dNFT being a badge of honor as a Boat Head yacht club member, it also holds value that increases based on your activity. Each dNFT is also encoded with easter eggs and other fun secrets you’ll unravel over time.

As a bonus, you can travel to real-world yacht clubs and level up your NFT. You receive a unique badge for every location you visit, otherwise known as a digital burgee flag. The value of your Boat Head dNFT increases for every yacht club you visit.

The dNFT also functions as a membership token, and is valued at $1,000 USDC or Ethereum Floor Price. Want to sell your membership to another Boat Head initiate? That’s also possible and they get the full rights of the previous holder’s earned rewards and digital yachting membership.

Problem

Yacht Clubs are too exclusive. They have massive wait lists to join with expensive annual membership rates. Usually you have to wait for members to die of old age for the wait lists to cycle through.

Because they operate at max capacity, they don't scale very well and as their members age, the activity around the club gradually becomes more and more stagnant.

Solution

Reciprocal membership is the best way for Yacht Clubs to sper on economic activity between one another

For first time ever, Yacht Clubs are opening their doors to an online community of an eccentric group of Boat heads. As the name implies Boat Heads are people that can't stop thinking and dreaming about boats. And they love to travel to new destinations and spend their money sampling the local food, cocktails and amenities when visiting a yacht club.

Economics

The Yacht Clubs can expense the BHYC treasury for any expenditures accumulated by the Boat Heads.

The BHYC Treasury is amassed by the original Mint of the Boat Head dNFTs. The Holders pay annual dues, or the dNFT goes back on the open market.

The BHYC takes a small percentage on a every transaction involving its dNFTs.
